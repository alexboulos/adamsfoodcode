Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What types of frozen appetizers do you offer under private label brands?

We offer a wide range of frozen appetizers under private label brands, including but not limited to spring rolls, samosas, sambousek, mini pizza, and more. Our products are customizable to meet your specific requirements and preferences.

2. How do you ensure the quality and safety of your frozen appetizers?

Quality and safety are our top priorities. Our facility is ISO 22000 certified, which means we adhere to stringent food safety and quality management standards. We implement rigorous quality control measures at every stage of production, from sourcing raw materials to the final product. Additionally, our team undergoes continuous training to stay updated on the latest food safety practices.

3. Can you accommodate special dietary requirements or preferences for private label products?

Yes, we can accommodate special dietary requirements and preferences. Whether you need gluten-free, vegan, or other specific options, our team works closely with you to develop products that meet your needs without compromising on taste or quality.
