Adams Food Co. (The Real Lebanese Taste) started as a family business in 2010. Due to market need, pledged to create a homemade style frozen natural appetizers/finger food concept for consumers, prepared with quality ingredients.

without any chemicals or artificial colorings for the whole family to enjoy morning, lunch, evening or snacking at all time, even for any type of event.

Adams Food uses 100% natural fresh & quality ingredients for its prepared frozen products presented to you and your family especially children as a snack at home or to school to have and enjoy.

Tradition and reliability sets Adams Food to the highest standards of frozen carefully prepared products and customer satisfaction.
